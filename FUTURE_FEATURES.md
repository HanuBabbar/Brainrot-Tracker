# Brainrot Tracker - Future Feature Ideas

Here is a collection of creative features to implement in the future to help reduce doomscrolling and build better habits:

1. **"Save the Brain" Virtual Pet (Gamification)**: A visual representation of a healthy pink brain that slowly gets "sick" (turns green, melts) as the user approaches their scrolling limit. If they stay under the limit, the brain levels up or earns accessories.

2. **Doomscroll Interventions**: 
   - *The "Breathe" Screen*: An overlay that forces a 5-10 second delay and a deep breathing exercise before opening TikTok or Instagram.
   - *Grayscale Mode Prompt*: Suggesting or automatically switching the screen to grayscale when approaching the daily limit to make content less stimulating.

3. **Head-to-Head Friend Battles & "Wall of Shame"**: 
   - *Duels*: Challenge friends to a 7-day "Focus Duel" to see who scrolls less.
   - *Wall of Shame*: A reversed leaderboard where the person with the highest "brainrot score" gets roasted with a dunce cap icon.

4. **Scroll Velocity & Session Tracking**: Tracking how *fast* a user swipes (indicating low attention span) or how long a single continuous session lasts, triggering a custom "Touch Grass" notification if a session exceeds 30 minutes.

5. **The "Dopamine Menu" (Alternative Activities)**: When hitting the daily limit, suggest quick offline tasks (e.g., drink water, 10 push-ups, text a friend) that grant a tiny bit of "grace period" scrolling when checked off.

6. **Aggressive Accountability**: 
   - *Hardcore Mode*: Sends increasingly aggressive and funny push notifications ("Your FBI agent is bored") when limits are blown past.
   - *Social Shaming*: Auto-generating a text message to send to an accountability partner confessing screen time.

7. **Lock Screen Widgets**: A widget showing a "Brainrot Level" percentage directly on the lock screen to stop a doomscrolling session before the phone is even unlocked.
